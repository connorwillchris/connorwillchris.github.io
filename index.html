Old-School Essentials

What is Old-School Essentials?

  • Simple Robust Rules Old-School Essentials has light, easily modified rules, putting the focus on play on shared imagination, improvisation, and fun.

Terminology

Basic Terminology

Referee
Also known as the Game Master (GM). The person who designs the game world and runs game sessions for other players, arbitrating the rules and determining the reactions of people and creatures encountered.

Players
Every other participant in the game. Each player usually runs a single character.

Player Characters
The imagined character played by a player.
