nunpa musi

Player characters are described, in game terms, by a set of statistics that define their abilities, strengths, and weaknesses in the game world.

jan nunpa

The basic physical and mental strengths and weaknesses of a character. There are 6 ability scores: wawa (W), sona (So), pilin (P), tawa (T), sijelo (Si), lawa (L). q A character is ranked in each ability score by a number between 3-18. (3 being the worst score possible and 18 the best.)
