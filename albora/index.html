Albora

Basic Premise

The world is set after a previous world (known simply as the "world before" or something similar) and their culture reflects this. The primary religion at the time believes that the Gods were partially responsible for the fall of the world so the Gods put a high emphasis on progress and remembering the past.

The world is inhabited by a bunch of human "species" that are inspired by old extinct human species, such as Homo-Habilis.
