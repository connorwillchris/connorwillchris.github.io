Races

Bugfolk

The default bug type in Ancient Crevasse. They typically have a shell or carapace and can apply themselves to most situations.

Adept. You gain an additional talent roll at 1st level.

Moth Tribe

The moth tribe is a tribe of winged and fuzzy bugs that are gentle, but stern.

Glide. You are able to glide when you fall, to a max of 50 in-game units.

Mantis

The Mantis Tribe are a skilled tribe of dexterous bug that pride themselves on their finesse and grace.

Extreeme Balance. You have advantage on Dexterity checks and get a +1 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons.

Beast Tribe
