What is Beyora

The setting made for Japanese players new to OSE.

Names and Language

This game setting is designed for Japanese people to get them interested in OSE.

Names

  • ishi: Stone
  • tora: Tiger
  • kumo: Spider
  • raikou: Thunder or thunder god
  • aka: red
  • tetsu: iron or steel
  • kage: shadow
  • hana: flower
  • kenji: strong and intelligent
  • raiju: thunder beast
  • katsu: victory or to win
  • goro: fifth son
  • sumi: ink
  • ikuto: to grow or thrive
  • hoshiko: child of the stars
