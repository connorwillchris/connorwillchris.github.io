Old Norse and English Translation

Old Norse

Þá mælti konungr: “Verið heilir, menn mínir.”

Þeir svara: “Heilr sé þú, herra.”

Síðan bjuggu þeir skip sín og héldu norðr um haf.

English

Then the king spoke: “Be well, my men.”

They answered: “Health to you, lord.”

After that they made ready their ships and sailed north across the sea.
