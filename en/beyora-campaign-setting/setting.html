What is Beyora

The Pitch:

  • There was an ancient cataclysm an age ago, and the world has now "reset." People now live in primative tribes. This world is in a late stone age.
  • Technology from civilizations past litter the land. This tech borders on the strange and magical.
  • Iron and iron weapons are antithetical to magic. Anyone who practices magic must avoid iron or else it damages them.

Names and Language

This game setting is designed for Japanese people to get them interested in OSE. It also includes conlangs that would be easy for a japanese person to understand.

Common Names

There are no family names, so a person is only given a first name.

A person may be given another name if their name conflicts with another, such as two "Aoi" names for example. So their name will be repeated: "Aoiaoi" for example.

  • ishi: Stone
  • tora: Tiger
  • kumo: Spider
  • raikou: Thunder or thunder god
  • aka: red
  • tetsu: iron or steel
  • kage: shadow
  • hana: flower
  • kenji: strong and intelligent
  • raiju: thunder beast
  • katsu: victory or to win
  • goro: fifth son
  • sumi: ink
  • ikuto: to grow or thrive
  • hoshiko: child of the stars
